How to Play

🐈‍⬆️ The cat runs on its own — you control the jumps.

⬆️ Tap (screen, mouse, SPACE, or ↑) to jump. Hold a touch a touch longer for a higher jump.

🚀 Tap again in the air to double-jump — the monkey flips for an extra boost.

🦦 Over a wide pit, a glowing vine hangs down. Hold as you reach it and the monkey’s tail grabs on and swings you across — let go to fling forward.

🍌 Grab banana-coins. Jump over crates and zappers; don’t jump into hanging zappers — run under them.

🛡️ Power-ups: Shield takes one hit, Magnet pulls coins in, Overcharge makes you briefly unstoppable.

🏁 Level 1 is an easy warm-up. Each level you clear gets faster and adds a new challenge (double-jump gaps, overhead hazards, then tail-swings).
