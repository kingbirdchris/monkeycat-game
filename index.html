How to Play

🐈‍⬆️ The cat runs the wall on its own — you handle the jumps.

⬆️ Tap (screen, mouse, SPACE, or ↑) to jump. Hold a touch longer for a higher jump.

🚀 Tap again in the air to double-jump — the monkey flips for an extra boost.

🌳 Over a wide gap in the wall, a glowing branch hangs down. Hold as you reach it and the monkey’s tail grabs on and swings you across — let go to fling forward.

🧊 Jump over potted cacti and garden gnomes. Watch the yards: homeowners throw shoes — jump them.

👮 Animal control is on your tail and flings nets — jump those too, or get hauled away.

🛡️ Power-ups: Shield takes one hit, Magnet pulls coins in, Overcharge makes you briefly unstoppable.

🏁 Level 1 is an easy warm-up. Each level gets faster and adds a new challenge.
